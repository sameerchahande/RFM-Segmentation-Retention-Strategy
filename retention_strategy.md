## Retention Actions by Segment

### 1. At-Risk Customers (649 customers) - PRIORITY 1
**Behavior:** Previously active, no purchase in 90+ days
**Action:** 
- Immediate win-back email with 15% discount
- Personalized product recommendations based on past purchases
- Survey to identify churn reasons
- Follow-up call for high-value at-risk customers
**Expected Value:** 10-15% reactivation rate, ~65 customers recovered

### 2. Champions (401 customers) - PRIORITY 2
**Behavior:** Recent, frequent, high-value purchasers
**Action:**
- Exclusive early access to new products
- VIP loyalty program enrollment
- Referral incentives (10% off for both referrer and referee)
- Personalized thank-you notes from CEO
**Expected Value:** 90%+ retention, ~361 customers retained

### 3. High-Value but Unhappy (33 customers) - PRIORITY 1
**Behavior:** High spenders with support tickets/returns
**Action:**
- Immediate personal call from customer success manager
- Service recovery compensation (20% discount or free product)
- Priority support line access
- Follow-up within 48 hours
**Expected Value:** 60%+ retention, ~20 customers recovered

### 4. Loyal Customers (339 customers) - PRIORITY 2
**Behavior:** Consistent purchasers, moderate frequency
**Action:**
- Cross-sell complementary products
- Loyalty points multiplier (2x on next purchase)
- Birthday/anniversary discounts
- Exclusive content (beauty tips, tutorials)
**Expected Value:** 85% retention, ~288 customers retained

### 5. Potential Loyalists (280 customers) - PRIORITY 2
**Behavior:** Moderate engagement, growth potential
**Action:**
- Engagement campaign (email, push notifications)
- First-purchase incentive if not yet bought
- Product education and use cases
- Social proof and reviews
**Expected Value:** 70% conversion to loyal, ~196 customers upgraded

### 6. New Customers (265 customers) - PRIORITY 3
**Behavior:** Recent first purchase, low frequency
**Action:**
- Welcome series (3 emails over 2 weeks)
- Product usage tips and tutorials
- Second-purchase incentive (10% off)
- Community invitation
**Expected Value:** 40% repeat purchase, ~106 customers retained

### 7. Dormant Customers (228 customers) - PRIORITY 3
**Behavior:** Inactive 6+ months, had purchase history
**Action:**
- Reactivation survey (what went wrong?)
- We-miss-you campaign with 20% discount
- New product announcements
- Re-engagement via multiple channels
**Expected Value:** 5-8% reactivation, ~11-18 customers recovered

### 8. Discount-Sensitive Customers (59 customers) - PRIORITY 4
**Behavior:** Frequent but low-value, high discount usage
**Action:**
- Tiered loyalty program (non-discount benefits)
- Bundle deals (value perception)
- Limited-time offers (urgency)
- Consider gradual discount reduction
**Expected Value:** 50% retention without discounts, ~30 customers retained

### 9. Lost Customers (228 customers) - PRIORITY 4
**Behavior:** No purchases, very high recency
**Action:**
- Final reactivation attempt (25% discount)
- Feedback survey
- Remove from active marketing (cost saving)
- Re-add to pool after 90 days
**Expected Value:** 2-3% reactivation, ~5-7 customers recovered
